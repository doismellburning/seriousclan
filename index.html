War Rules

Donations

  • Set your donation message for your clan war castle
  • Only donate what's requested in other people's donate message. If they've not changed it from the default, wait.
  • If there are less than 6 hours left in preparation day and they haven't set their donation message, donate wizards / archers

Attacks

  • Don't TH snipe
  • Do not attack a base that has already been 3-starred.
  • Do not attack a base that you have no hope of 2-starring (unless everything else has been 3-starred)
  • Aim to attack the highest level base you could reasonably 2-star, unless you could improve stars elsewhere
